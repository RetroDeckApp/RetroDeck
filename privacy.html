RetroDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2025

1. Introduction

RetroDeck (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website, associated subdomains (collectively, the “Service”), and the RetroDeck iOS application distributed via the Apple App Store. By accessing or using the Service, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to this policy as well as our Terms of Service. RetroDeck currently does not offer an Android application.

2. Definitions and Key Terms

To keep things clear, the following terms have specific meanings whenever used in this policy:

  • Cookie: Small data stored by your browser to identify you, enable analytics, and remember preferences.
  • Company: “RetroDeck App,” responsible for your information under this privacy policy.
  • Country: The jurisdiction where RetroDeck operates; for this policy, that country is Turkey.
  • Customer: Any organization or individual using RetroDeck to manage relationships with their own users.
  • Device: Any internet-connected device such as a phone, tablet, or computer used to access RetroDeck.
  • IP address: A number assigned to devices connected to the internet, often indicating approximate location.
  • Personnel: Employees and contractors engaged by RetroDeck.
  • Personal Data: Information that can directly or indirectly identify a natural person.
  • Service: RetroDeck’s website, app, and related offerings described in this policy.
  • Third-party service: Partners or providers that supply content or functionality we believe may interest you.
  • Website: RetroDeck’s site at https://retrodeck.app and related subdomains.
  • You: Individuals or entities registered with RetroDeck to use the Service.

3. Information Automatically Collected

When you access RetroDeck, we automatically gather certain information such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, time zone, and operating system. We may also collect clickstream data (pages viewed, searches performed), cookie identifiers, and support call details to maintain security and improve service quality.

4. Collection of Personal Data and Method

We process personal data only for the purposes defined in this policy. The information we may collect includes:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number (when you contact support)
  • Facial feature data or photos that you upload to RetroDeck
  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and Identifier for Vendors/Developers (IDFV) associated with your device
  • IP address

We collect this data when you register, interact with the Service, contact us, or explicitly grant RetroDeck access to information stored on your device.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Deliver AI photo generation, face swaps, and related Service features
  • Manage accounts and provide customer support
  • Enable access to app functionality and updates
  • Communicate about your account, Service changes, and policies
  • Process payments for premium services, where applicable
  • Perform analytics to improve performance, safety, and reliability

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including encryption, secure transmission, and restricted access, to protect your data. While we strive for robust security, no method of internet transmission or storage is completely secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your devices updated.

7. Data Sharing

RetroDeck does not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who operate on our behalf, legal authorities when required by law, or other parties with your explicit consent.

8. Third-Party Access and Services

We may use aggregated and anonymized data to improve our services. RetroDeck integrates third-party technologies within the iOS app, each with their own privacy policies. Review their policies for more information:

  • Firebase Services (Analytics, Analytics Core, Analytics Identity, App Check, Authentication, Realtime Database, Firestore, Firestore Combine, Remote Config, Storage, Firebase AI): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy. These services help us measure usage, protect the app from abuse, manage accounts, sync and store user data, and deliver AI-powered experiences.
  • Google Generative AI: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We rely on Google’s generative models to power optional image creation features.
  • RevenueCat (including RevenueCatUI): https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/. RevenueCat manages our in-app purchases, entitlement tracking, and subscription billing.

We may disclose user-provided or automatically collected information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, investigate fraud, respond to government requests, or with service providers who agree to adhere to this policy.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand usage patterns and deliver core functionality. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but some features may not function properly as a result.

11. Children’s Privacy

RetroDeck is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.

13. App Store Refund Requests Consent

When you request a refund through the App Store for purchases made within RetroDeck, we may share usage details with Apple to help them evaluate the request. This may include consumption data, timestamps, and other relevant information about your use of the purchased content. We share this data only for processing the specific refund request and do not share it with other third parties.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at retrodeckapp@outlook.com.
